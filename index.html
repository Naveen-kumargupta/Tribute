Mahendra singh DHONI

Former Indian captain (2004-2019)

Best Finisher in the World

Mahendra Singh Dhoni was born on 7th July 1981 in Ranchi, Jharkhand (at that time Ranchi was a part of Bihar), India. Mahi is the nickname of MS Dhoni. He was born in a middle-class Hindu Rajput family. His father's name is Paan Singh Dhoni and his mother's name is Devaki Devi. Dhoni's father worked in MECON as a junior manager. Dhoni has two siblings. He has a sister, Jayanti, and a brother, names Narendra Singh Dhoni.
